## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes a Global Superstore dataset to uncover revenue trends, profitability patterns, and discount impact on margins — helping businesses make smarter pricing and inventory decisions.

## 📂 Dataset Details

- 📦 **9,000+ rows** of sales records
- 👥 **1,590** Total Customers
- 🗂️ **3 Categories:** Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology
- 🌐 **Markets:** Africa, APAC, Canada, EMEA, EU, LATAM, US
- 📅 **Years:** 2011 – 2014

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 💰 **Total Sales: $12.62M** | Total Profit: **$1.46M**
- 💻 **Technology** leads in both sales ($47.44M) and profit ($6.63M)
- 📈 Sales grew consistently 2011–2014, but profit growth is slower due to heavy discounts
- 🏷️ **Average Discount: 0.14** — some products sold at a loss
- 📦 **Medium priority orders** dominate profit contribution
- 🗺️ Regional performance varies significantly across global markets
- 🏆 **Top Sub-categories:** Phones, Machines, and Chairs

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- ✅ Reduce discounts on low-margin products
- ✅ Focus marketing efforts on Technology category
- ✅ Investigate and reprice loss-making products
- ✅ Replicate high-performing region strategies globally

---

## 📈 Impact

> Identified discount-driven margin loss and category-level profitability gaps — insights that can directly inform inventory and pricing strategy decisions.
